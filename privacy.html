Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Applies to the StudyNest Chrome Extension

Short version: StudyNest stores your study data locally on your device. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties. AI features send only what you type to process your request — nothing else.

1. What data we collect

StudyNest collects and stores the following data to provide its core features:

  • Study data — tasks, highlights, flashcards, notes, and study progress
  • Usage data — focus session durations, streak counts, XP points, and Pomodoro history
  • Preferences — language settings, theme preferences, blocked sites list
  • Calendar data — tasks and deadlines you add to the study calendar
  • Account data (optional) — email address and name if you create an account to sync across devices

2. Where your data is stored

By default, all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). This means your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly use a feature that requires it.

If you create an account, your data is synced to our secure servers to enable cross-device access. This sync data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

3. AI features and data processing

StudyNest includes an AI assistant powered by third-party AI services. When you use AI features:

  • The text you type or highlighted content you select is sent to our backend server
  • Our server forwards it to the AI provider to generate a response
  • We do not store your AI conversations permanently
  • We do not use your conversations to train AI models

AI requests are processed solely to generate the response you requested.

4. What we do NOT do

  • ❌ We do not sell your personal data to anyone
  • ❌ We do not share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing history
  • ❌ We do not read the content of pages you visit (except text you explicitly highlight using our tool)
  • ❌ We do not display ads

5. Permissions we request and why

  • storage — To save your highlights, tasks, flashcards, and settings locally
  • tabs — To detect which site you're on for the focus/blocking feature
  • activeTab — To allow text highlighting on the current page
  • scripting — To inject the highlight tool when you select text
  • notifications — To send Pomodoro timer alerts
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — Required so the focus mode can block any domain you add to your blocked list

6. How to delete your data

You can remove all StudyNest data at any time by:

  • Going to Chrome Settings → Extensions → StudyNest → Remove — this deletes all local data
  • Or inside the extension: Settings → Reset Data
  • If you have an account, email us to request complete account deletion

7. Children's privacy

StudyNest is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our features evolve. We will notify users of significant changes through the extension or our website. Continued use of StudyNest after changes means you accept the updated policy.
