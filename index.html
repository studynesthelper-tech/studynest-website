Questions

Frequently asked

Is StudyNest really free?

Yes. The core features — planner, focus mode, highlights, flashcards, progress tracking, and AI chat (limited) — are completely free forever. Premium unlocks unlimited AI and advanced analytics.

Does it work on all websites?

Yes. StudyNest works across all websites — Wikipedia, course platforms, news sites, PDFs in Chrome, Google Docs, and more. The highlight and summarize feature works anywhere you can select text.

How is the AI powered?

StudyNest uses state-of-the-art large language models to power its AI chat, summarization, and flashcard generation. Your data is processed securely and never sold to third parties.

Can I use it without creating an account?

Absolutely. Install the extension and start using it immediately. Creating an account lets you sync your data across devices and unlocks the full feature set.

Is my study data private?

Yes. Your highlights, notes, and flashcards are stored locally on your device by default. Cloud sync is fully encrypted. We never sell your data.
